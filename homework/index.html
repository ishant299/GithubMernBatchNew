IBM (International business machine)

IBM is a global technology and consulting company that helps organizations use advanced digital solutions to improve their work and business operations. It provides services in areas like cloud computing, artificial intelligence, data analytics, cybersecurity, and software development. IBM works with businesses, governments, and institutions to solve complex problems using technology. The company is also known for its research and innovation, especially in AI and quantum computing. Overall, IBM plays an important role in shaping the modern digital world by creating tools and services that make businesses smarter, faster, and more efficient.

About Us

IBM (International Business Machines Corporation), founded in 1911 in the United States, is a leading global technology company that develops computers, software, cloud computing, artificial intelligence like Watson, and provides IT and consulting services to businesses across more than 170 countries, with its headquarters in Armonk, New York.
